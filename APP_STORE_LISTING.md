## Description (4000 chars max)

BidDrop is the all-in-one sales tool built for home improvement contractors. Canvass neighborhoods, build instant estimates, and send professional letters and postcards — all from your phone.

**CANVASS YOUR TERRITORY**
Drop pins on homes as you walk neighborhoods. Mark each property with a status (Needs Roof, Solar Lead, Interested, Not Home, and more), add notes, snap photos, and track your team's progress on a live satellite map.

**INSTANT MULTI-TRADE ESTIMATES**
Build accurate estimates in under 60 seconds for Roofing, Solar, Fencing, Siding, Gutters, Insulation, Exterior Paint, Doors, and Windows. Satellite roof measurements auto-populate your estimate — no tape measure needed.

**PROFESSIONAL LETTERS & POSTCARDS**
Generate a branded, personalized letter or postcard for every homeowner — automatically populated with their address, your estimate, financing options, and your company info. Send physical mail directly from the app via our postcard fulfillment service.

**SATELLITE ROOF & SOLAR ANALYSIS**
Every address gets an instant satellite analysis: roof area, squares, pitch, shading quality, and solar potential (system size, panel count, annual output). Powered by Google's Project Sunroof data.

**TEAM MANAGEMENT**
Manage your canvassing crew from the Agency dashboard. See every rep's pins in real time, track performance, and assign territories.

**WORKS FOR YOUR WHOLE TEAM**
- Field reps canvass and pin homes
- Sales reps build estimates and send letters at the door
- Managers track territory coverage and lead volume

No door-knocking. No clipboards. No wasted trips. BidDrop puts your entire sales process in your pocket.
